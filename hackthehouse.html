Live sensor feed?

Indeed! There are at least 7 of our reelceivers permanently installed in the house. And their live sensor feed is openly accessible from our cloud platform via:

  • a REST API
  • websockets

What do the sensors sense?

Each reelceiver detects Bluetooth devices that are in range. This includes devices such as:

  • smartphones
  • wearables
  • beacons
  • laptops
  • Smart TVs
  • etc.

What does the platform provide?

For devices which support Bluetooth Smart (aka BLE or Bluetooth 4.x) and send advertising packets, our platform will:

  • locate which sensor they're nearest
  • identify them uniquely
  • decode the data they send
  • timestamp their activity
